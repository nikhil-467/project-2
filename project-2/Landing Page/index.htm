Top Engineering College

Gokaraju Rangaraju Institute of Engineering and Technology (GRIET) is established in 1997 by Dr. G Gangaraju as a self-financed institute under the aegis of Gokaraju Rangaraju Educational Society. GRIET is approved by AICTE, New Delhi, permanently affiliated to and autonomous under JNTUH, Hyderabad.

Our Global Campus

Our Facilities

Gokaraju Rangaraju Institute of Engineering and Technology (GRIET) in Hyderabad has a variety of facilities, including:

GRIET Library

GRIET library and Information Centre supports teaching-learning and research activities. It consists of central library and department libraries. Central library stores resources of all departments whereas department libraries store resources of departments. The library is kept open round the year.

Play Ground

Sports and Games are an exciting and essential components for over all development, has created an elaborate infrastructure for them. The college has a well developed Basket Ball court, and several volley ball courts and Throw Ball courts and an internal Table Tennis Stadium.
